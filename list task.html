Chocolate Milkshake

Chocolate milkshake also known as choco milkshake is a popular drink with all age groups. There are many methods of preparing chocolate milkshake. Put the ingredients into the jar, just blend it and pour it into the glass and garnish the milkshake.

Ingredients

  • Glass
  • Milk
  • Chocolate Syrup
  • Whipped Cream
  • Vannila Icecream

Preparations

  1. Pour the milk into the blended jar.
  2. Add vannila Icecream into the jar.
  3. Add Chocolate syrup (Whatever flavours you like) into the jar.
  4. Blended it until it become frothy and there is no lumps of Icecream.
  5. Pour the mixture into the Glass and garnish it with Whipped Cream.
  6. Milkshake is ready, drink it and enjoy.
